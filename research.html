    Inspire, Educate, Enrich

    Use your talent to impact our community

    Purpose

    Purpose of research team is to use their talent in STEM subjects to educate, enrich, and motivate our community about current STEM fields and their new findings. Through working in research team, you will learn the importance of researching current fields and develop new skills such as data analysis, consensus of different research, and collaboration skills. Also, if you are in research team - college, you will research different colleges and their specialties in STEM. You will not only learn this information yourself but also motivates our next generation on STEM fields.

    Learn More

    Commitment

    You can work by yourself or teams of 2 or 3 to research about current STEM fields and their new discovery. Then, you will write a summary of each findings to general public with easier words because scientific research can be sometimes hard to understand. If the research has a data table, then you will make a new graph with the data to confirm the data analysis and use different perspective to understand the materials. Commitment will be 1-2 hours per week, and you will finish one research about current STEM fields (discovery) / college per 2-3 weeks.

    Learn More

    Benefits

    You will earn volunteer service hours for the research because through this proces, you are helping our community to understand current fields and motivate young children to learn about STEM. Also, our board will have new position for leadership: Head of Research. If you show great work ethics and commitment, we will select you as a Head of Research.

    Can you become both intern and research team?

    Yes, you can work as both intern and be part of our research team.
